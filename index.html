About

FlexureX Lab focuses on the design, nonlinear analysis, and experimental validation of advanced compliant mechanisms that enable sub-micron precision motion. Our work supports next-generation nano-positioning systems, surgical tools, and precision robotics through innovations in flexure-based actuation and structural optimization.

Research Highlights

  • Nonlinear design of general single-translation constraints and spherical joints (J. Mech. Des., 2025).
  • Modeling of general lumped-compliance beams (Int. J. Mech. Sci., 2024).
  • Over-constraint compliant mechanisms with nearly constant amplification ratios (MMT, 2023).
  • Mirror-symmetrical XY compliant parallel manipulator minimizing parasitic rotation (J. Mech. Des., 2022).
